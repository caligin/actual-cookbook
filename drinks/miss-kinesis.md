# miss-kinesis

a "kina" take on a white lady.

## ingredients

- 45ml london dry gin
- 22.5ml lime juice
- 22.5ml kina l'areo d'or
- 15ml simple syrup
- 1 egg white
- orange bitters

## preparation

dry shake everything except bitters. garnish with bitters drops on the foam.

## notes

a "stronger" gin is preferrable, like boatyard double gin.
