# casino

from totr [5 EASY GIN COCKTAIL RECIPES!](https://youtu.be/u-fzCODNVUw)

## ingredients

- 60ml gin
- 15ml maraschino
- 22.5ml lemon juice
- 7.5ml simple syrup
- 3 dashes orange bitters
- maraschino cherry

## preparation

shake all except cherry. double strain, garnish with cherry
