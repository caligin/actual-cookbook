# ssamjang

this is the heavenly sauce that accompanies a korean bbq meal.

can never have enough of it!

serves 2.

## ingredients

- 2 cloves garlic
- 2 chillies
- 2spn gochujang
- 2spn miso
- 1spn honey
- 1spn sesame oil
- 1spn gochugaru

## preparation

crush the garlic and mince the chilli, then mix all ingredients together
