# kimchijeon

from: https://www.maangchi.com/recipe/kimchijeon

## ingredients

- 260g chopped kimchi
- 3spn kimchi juice
- half an onion
- 1cspn salt
- 1cspn sugar
- 80g flour
- 60ml water

## preparation

chop onion, mix everything in a bowl. set a pan on high and pour the mix, spreading it thinly. cook until crispy (1-2 minutes per side)
